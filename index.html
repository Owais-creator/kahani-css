The Thirsty Crow

Crow flying in the sky

It was a very hot summer day. A thirsty crow was flying here and there in search of water. He had been flying for a long time and was getting tired.


Crow sees the pot

At last, the crow saw a pitcher lying under a tree. He became very happy and quickly flew down to see if there was some water inside it.


Crow looking inside the pot

The crow looked inside the pitcher. There was a little water at the bottom, but the neck of the pitcher was too narrow. The poor crow tried to reach the water, but he could not


Crow collecting stones

The clever crow thought for a while. Suddenly, he got a smart idea! He started picking up small stones from the ground and dropping them into the pitcher one by one.


Crow dropping stones in pot

As the crow dropped more and more stones into the pitcher, the water level began to rise higher and higher. The crow kept working patiently..


Crow drinking water

At last, the water reached the top of the pitcher. The thirsty crow drank the cool water happily, felt refreshed, and flew away into the sky..
